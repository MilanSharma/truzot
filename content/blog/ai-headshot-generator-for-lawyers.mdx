## Tips for Best Results

### Photo Guidelines

- **Lighting**: Natural light near a window works best
- **Expression**: Neutral, friendly expression
- **Background**: Simple, uncluttered background
- **Clothing**: Professional attire you'd wear to client meetings
- **Variety**: Include slight angle variations and different lighting

### Style Selection

- **Match your firm's brand**: Choose styles that align with your firm's visual identity
- **Consider your practice area**: Corporate lawyers may prefer formal styles, while family law attorneys might benefit from approachable looks
- **Think about usage**: Select styles appropriate for each platform (website vs LinkedIn vs social media)
