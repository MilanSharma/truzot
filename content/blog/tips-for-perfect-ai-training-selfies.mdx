The quality of your AI-generated professional headshots depends almost entirely on the photos you upload to train the model. Just like a traditional photoshoot, preparation makes all the difference between average results and stunning professional headshots.

Here are five expert tips to get the best results from any AI headshot generator:

## 1. Use Natural Lighting

Window light is your best friend when taking selfies for AI headshot training. Stand facing a large window and avoid overhead fluorescent lights that create unflattering shadows. Natural light brings out your skin tone naturally and gives the AI clear information about your facial features.

The golden hours (just after sunrise or before sunset) provide the most flattering natural light. However, any well-lit room with indirect sunlight will work well for AI headshot training photos.

## 2. Capture Multiple Angles

AI headshot generators need to understand your facial structure from all angles to produce natural-looking results. Take selfies from:

- Straight on (facing the camera directly)
- Three-quarter turns (slightly to the left and right)
- Slight profile views

This variety gives the AI enough data to understand your facial structure, bone structure, and how your features look from different perspectives. The result is AI headshots that look like you from every angle.

## 3. Vary Your Expressions

Include serious, smiling, and neutral expressions in your training photos. This helps the AI generate natural-looking professional headshots that capture your personality.

You'll want headshots with a warm, approachable smile for LinkedIn and other professional profiles, but you may also want a few more serious, executive-style photos for company pages or speaking engagements. By providing varied expressions in your training photos, you give the AI the flexibility to produce both.

## 4. Avoid Busy Backgrounds

Plain walls or simple backgrounds work best for AI headshot training photos. The AI needs to focus on your face, not the clutter behind you. A blank wall, a simple doorway, or an outdoor space with a plain background are all excellent choices.

The beauty of AI headshot generation is that the background in your training photos doesn't matter for your final results — the AI will replace backgrounds with professional options like offices, outdoor locations, and creative studios. But a clean training photo helps the AI isolate your face more accurately.

## 5. Wear Different Outfits

Change clothes between training shots to give the AI more flexibility when generating your final headshots. If you always wear the same shirt, your AI headshots will be limited in variety.

Try a collared shirt or blazer for corporate-style headshots, a simple solid color for LinkedIn profiles, and something more casual for relaxed professional settings. The AI can then generate headshots in all these styles.

## Bonus Tip: Use High-Resolution Photos

Use your phone's highest resolution setting when taking training photos. More pixels mean more data for the AI to work with, resulting in sharper, more detailed AI headshots. Most modern smartphones take photos that are more than sufficient for professional AI headshot generation.
