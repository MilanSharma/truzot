## What Are AI Headshots?

AI headshots are professional-quality portrait photos generated by artificial intelligence. You upload a few casual selfies (typically 1-5 photos), and the AI analyzes your facial features — including your face shape, skin tone, eye color, and bone structure. It then generates a collection of professional headshots in various styles, backgrounds, and outfits.

The underlying technology uses a type of AI model called a LoRA (Low-Rank Adaptation) that's trained on your face using the photos you provide. This allows the AI to generate realistic images of you in settings you've never actually been in — corporate offices, outdoor locations, creative studios, and more.

## How to Get the Best AI Headshots

### Choose the Right Photos
The quality of your AI headshots depends heavily on the photos you upload. Use these guidelines:

- Take photos in natural light near a window
- Capture multiple angles (front, three-quarter, profile)
- Show different expressions (smiling, neutral, serious)
- Avoid busy backgrounds
- Wear different outfits across photos

### Select Appropriate Styles
Choose styles that match your industry and personal brand. A corporate lawyer needs Conservative Executive styles, while a graphic designer might benefit from Creative and Editorial options.

### Set Your Preferences
Provide accurate details about your appearance — eye color, hair color, and existing clothing choices help the AI generate more realistic results.
