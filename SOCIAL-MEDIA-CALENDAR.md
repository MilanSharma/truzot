## Content Templates

### LinkedIn Carousel Template

1. **Hook slide:** Bold statement or question
2. **Problem slide:** Why this matters
3. **Solution slide:** How to fix it
4. **Example slide:** Before/after or case study
5. **CTA slide:** Clear call to action

### Instagram Reel Template

1. **Hook (0-3s):** Attention-grabbing visual
2. **Problem (3-10s):** Show the issue
3. **Solution (10-25s):** Demonstrate the fix
4. **Result (25-30s):** Show transformation
5. **CTA (30-35s):** Link in bio

### Twitter Thread Template

1. **Hook tweet:** Strong opening statement
2. **Context tweets:** 3-5 educational points
3. **Proof tweet:** Statistic or example
4. **CTA tweet:** Clear action

## Crisis Management

### Negative Comment Response Protocol

1. Acknowledge quickly (within 1 hour)
2. Empathize with concern
3. Offer solution publicly
4. Move detailed conversation to DM
5. Follow up to resolution

### Content Moderation

- Hide spam comments immediately
- Address legitimate concerns professionally
- Document recurring issues for product improvement
- Maintain brand voice in all responses
