## Marketing Channels & Tactics

### 1. SEO (Already Strong - Expand)

#### Programmatic SEO Expansion

- **Current:** 8 professions, 13 cities, 6 styles = ~104 combo pages
- **Expansion Goal:** 20 professions, 50 cities, 10 styles = 10,000+ pages
- **Priority:** High-value combos (LinkedIn + major cities)

#### Content Marketing

- **Blog Cadence:** 2x/week minimum
- **Topics:**
  - LinkedIn profile tips
  - Headshot photography guides
  - Industry-specific advice
  - Before/after case studies
  - AI technology education

#### Comparison Pages

- **Current:** 4 competitor alternatives
- **Expansion:** Target top 20 competitors
- **Format:** Detailed feature/pricing comparison tables

### 2. Social Media (New - High Priority)

#### LinkedIn Strategy

- **Why:** Primary platform for headshot users
- **Tactics:**
  - Post daily tips about LinkedIn photos
  - Share before/after transformations (with permission)
  - Engage in #headshot #professionalbranding discussions
  - Run LinkedIn ads targeting job seekers
  - Create LinkedIn-specific landing page

#### Instagram/TikTok Strategy

- **Content Style:** Visual transformations, behind-the-scenes
- **Tactics:**
  - Reels showing AI generation process
  - Before/after compilations
  - User testimonials
  - Trending audio with headshot tips
  - Influencer partnerships

#### Twitter/X Strategy

- **Content:** Quick tips, industry news, engagement
- **Tactics:**
  - Daily headshot tips thread
  - Engage with career coaches
  - Share blog content
  - Twitter Spaces on personal branding

### 3. Email Marketing (New - High Priority)

## Risk Mitigation

### Potential Risks

1. **Ad costs increase** → Focus on organic channels
2. **Algorithm changes** → Diversify traffic sources
3. **Competitor copying** → Focus on brand and experience
4. **Technical issues** → Monitor uptime and performance
5. **Budget constraints** → Start with organic, scale with paid

### Contingency Plans

- If ads underperform → Shift budget to content SEO
- If social doesn't convert → Focus on email marketing
- If SEO takes time → Accelerate paid acquisition
- If budget runs out → Focus on organic partnerships

---

## Conclusion

This marketing strategy focuses on:

1. **Leveraging existing SEO foundation** (quick wins)
2. **Building owned audiences** (email, social)
3. **Diversifying acquisition channels** (reduce dependency)
4. **Data-driven optimization** (measure and iterate)
5. **Sustainable growth** (long-term focus over short-term hacks)

The key to success is consistent execution across multiple channels while continuously optimizing based on performance data.
